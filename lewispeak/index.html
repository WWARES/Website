Lewis Peak Amateur Repeater Association

Serving Amateur Radio Emergency Communications in Walla Walla County

Purpose

Provide voice and digital communication on VHF/UHF for emergency support of Walla Walla County’s Office of Emergency Management. Support the Walla Walla County Amateur Radio Emergency Service.
